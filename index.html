IGIRANEZA Joseph is dedicated to improving the lives of street children and vulnerable women who survived the Genocide.

IGIRA CULTURAL CENTER it is an organisation which works with the children from street and children from vulnurable families
we support this children through our cultural activities like Drumming, traditional dance and music.


We brought them together and taught them our cultural activities in order to develope their talents and rise money to support the Education,health
and their everyday life.

The mission of IGIRA CULTURAL CENTER

The mission of IGIRA CULTURAL CENTER is to empower children living in poverty street by providing them
Lessons in traditional Rwanda dance, music ,drumming and supporting them academically with school fees.

The IGIRA Foundation consists kids,single poverty women who survived Genocide and Youth who faced the same struggles when
were young and we want to give back to our community.


Each of us is dedicated to sharing the beautiful songs, drumming, and dancing with the children who live to our center.

Your donation and supporting allow the children to attend school and single poverty women help them to change their everyday life, to have clean and well-fitting
